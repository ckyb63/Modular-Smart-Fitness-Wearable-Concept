New Concept Technology

The Modular Revolution

Fitmodular is a new concept in fitness wearables. It is a modular system that allows you to build your perfect fitness companion by selecting and combining specialized sensor modules.

Core Advantages

  • Build Your Device: Choose only the sensors you need for your specific fitness goals
  • Evolve Over Time: Add new modules as your fitness journey progresses
  • Future-Proof Investment: Never buy a whole new device again - just upgrade modules
  • Sustainable Choice: Dramatically reduce e-waste through modular design
  • Cost Efficiency: Pay only for the features you actually use

Core Health Module

Essential vitals tracking including heart rate, steps, and basic activity monitoring.

Performance Module

Advanced metrics for serious athletes including VO2 max, training load, muscle activation, and recovery analysis.

Sleep & Recovery Module

Comprehensive sleep tracking with REM analysis, sleep quality scoring, and recovery recommendations.

Environmental Module

Track external factors like temperature, humidity, UV exposure, and air quality.
